Chivolo

A video app for kids that reflects the diversity of the real world.

When I reviewed my four-year-old stepdaughter's viewing history on the YouTube Kids app, I found that very few videos featured characters of color. I created Chivolo with content from the YouTube Data API to provide families with kids' videos that reflect the diversity of the real world, helping children connect with characters who look like themselves and form positive associations with people of all races.

Bio

In my twenties, I launched a clothing brand inspired by my grandmother's fabric business and organic cotton farmers in Peru, where I spend a fair amount of time when I'm not in my beloved home base of Portland, Oregon.

I credit entrepreneurship with my cultivation of digital marketing skills and my discovery of the joys of computer programming. Enticed by the power of web development, I enrolled in a coding bootcamp and reveled in a newfound form of creativity.

When I'm not coding, I manage social media profiles for mission-driven businesses, blog about women's clothing with pockets, and plot my next trip to a tropical rainforest.
